Special Black Coffee

Our black coffee is brewed from premium, single-origin beans to bring out the bold flavors and rich aroma. It's a perfect choice for coffee enthusiasts who appreciate the pure, unadulterated essence of coffee.

Croissant

Our café’s croissants are baked fresh daily, using high-quality butter for a perfectly flaky, golden crust and a soft, airy interior. They are crafted with care to deliver an authentic taste of France, pairing beautifully with your favorite coffee.

Choco Lava Cake

At our café, we craft Choco Lava Cakes with a rich, moist chocolate base filled with a luscious, molten chocolate center that oozes out with every bite. Baked fresh to perfection, it's a dessert indulgence you won't want to miss

Beer-Battered Fish and Chips

At our café, we prepare Beer-Battered Fish and Chips by coating fresh fish fillets in a light, crisp beer batter and frying them to golden perfection. Paired with hand-cut fries and served with a zesty tartar sauce, it's a comforting classic with a gourmet twist!

Matcha Lattes

At our café, we create Matcha Lattes using premium ceremonial-grade matcha whisked to a smooth, vibrant froth. Blended with creamy milk and a touch of sweetness, it offers a perfect harmony of earthy and velvety flavors in every sip.

Craft Sodas

At our café, we craft sodas by blending fresh fruit purees, house-made syrups, and sparkling water to create refreshing, fizzy drinks. Each flavor is uniquely balanced, offering a delightful mix of sweetness, tang, and effervescence.
